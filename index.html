What is Weather?
Weather refers to the atmospheric conditions in a specific location at a given time. It includes temperature, humidity, wind speed, air pressure, and precipitation such as rain, snow, or hail. Weather can change rapidly due to various natural factors like the sun, wind patterns, and ocean currents.


Why is Weather Important?
Weather plays a crucial role in our daily lives. It affects our clothing choices, travel plans, agriculture, and even our health. Extreme weather conditions, such as storms and heatwaves, can have a significant impact on communities and economies.


How is Weather Forecasted?
Meteorologists use advanced tools like satellites, radar systems, and weather balloons to predict weather conditions. By analyzing data such as air pressure, wind patterns, and humidity levels, they can provide accurate forecasts.
